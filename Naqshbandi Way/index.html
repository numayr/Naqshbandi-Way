Introduction




O wandering soul! Drowned in anxieties. Roaming around with a diseased heart. Welcome to the way.

I introduce to you this website, which I created for people like myself. To bring some light into your lives of darkness. To bring some happiness to the sad ones. To enlighten the hearts and fill them with divine love and light.

I tried to gather some resources, general awrad, adhkar, Du'as, Salawat which will be helpful in your journey, your battle against the ego. Al-Qur'an, Dhikr, Salawat, Awrad will be your guardians and your way to felicity in this world and the next. In Sha' Allah!
